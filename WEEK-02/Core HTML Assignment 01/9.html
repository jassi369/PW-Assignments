1. Strong Tag
    • Use it for emphasizing important words or phrases in your content.
    • Avoid overusing it, as excessive bold text can be visually overwhelming.
  2. Em tag
    • Use it for adding slight emphasis to specific words or phrases.
    • Similar to strong, avoid using it excessively, as too much italicized text can be distracting.
  3. Underline tag
    • Use it sparingly and for specific cases where underlining is necessary, such as indicating hyperlinks.
    • Avoid using it for general emphasis, as underlined text can be misinterpreted as links.
  4. Italic
    • Use to indicate emphasis or to differentiate between words or phrases
    • Can be used for book or movie titles, foreign words or phrases, or to denote thoughts or quotes within a sentence
