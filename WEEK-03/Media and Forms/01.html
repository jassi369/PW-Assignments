PW SKILLS

PW Skills is an online learning platform that offers courses on various tech topics, including coding, data science, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. The courses are taught by industry experts and are designed to help students learn the skills they need to succeed in the tech industry.

PW LABS

PW Labs is a cloud-based platform that provides students with access to unlimited computing power and resources for coding, data science, and artificial intelligence projects. The platform is designed to help students learn by doing, and it offers a variety of tools and features to help them build and deploy their projects.
