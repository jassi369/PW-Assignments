2. What are HTML entities? List out 5 commonly used HTML entities.
    Html entities are strings of text that start with an & and end with a semicolon;
    They are used to show characters that are reserved and imperceptible character.
     
     5 commonly used HTML entities are.
     
     1. &COPY;  -  copyright
     2. &AMP - Ampersand
     3. &GT  - Greater than >
     4. &TRADE; - trademark symbol
     5. &reg; - Registration mark
