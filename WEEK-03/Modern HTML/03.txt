3. What is web accessibility? List some of the assistive devices which play a major
    role in providing accessibility.

Web accessibility is practice of designing and developing web applications that can 
easily used and accessed by people with disabilities or different needs, without barriers or limitations.

Assistive devices which play a major role in providing accessibility.

1. Screen reader - This software reads out the content of a web page to individals
who are visually impaired. It can also communicate information about graphics and other elements.

2. Voice recognition software - Voice recognition software enables users to navigate web pages
 and input text using voice commands.

 3. Keyboard alternative - They are used in place of keyboard and are sipi and puff devices
 , head-tracking devices. and eye tracking devices allow individuals with physical disabilities to navigate 
 and interact with web pages without the use of a traditional keyboard or mouse.
